Society of Saint Matthew the Apostle

The Society of Saint Matthew the Apostle is an association with the mission to keep and further the Christian faith through prayer and the intercession of Saint Matthew.


The Society also sponsors and supports churches, clergy, and efforts to advance Christian faith and culture.


The Society is registered civilly as a nonprofit corporation.
